Videos

A collection of various video projects I've worked on for both college courses and personal enjoyment.

MADVILLIAN
A "fingerboarding" skate video submitted for my final in Video Art 2. It is meant to replicate the style of 90s skate videos. Filmed, edited and tricks done by me.

Ethan Unplugged
A documentary of my good friend, Ethan Johnson. An aspiring music producer, who has deep roots in music from his family, reflects on what his college experience meant to his career.

Factory Food
This a piece done in response to the prompt "fairytale in the supermarket". The video uses the juxtaposition of casual grocery store music with the utilitarian rumbling of the factory to make a commentary on how American food is manufactured. This point can be seen in the use of shots of items on shelves and as well as in the factory where the products are being made.

anywhere but here
Made for my Video Art 1 class during the heat of covid, this piece encapsulates my feelings about working in a dead end retail job.

Axe Murder
A short editing assignment made for Video Art 1.

Passing Out Pieces
A music video for Mac Demarco's "Passing Out Pieces". This was for an editing techniques assignment in my video art class, but also for personal enjoyment.

Levitate 2018
A short recap of my time at the Levitate Music and Art Festival 2018.
