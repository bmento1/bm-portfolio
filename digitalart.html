Digital Art

A collection of my digital art pieces created using Photoshop and Illustrator.

Cover Art Collaborations - 2019

These are all a part of a personal project that I did that incorporates some of my favorite album covers. Each picture is a combination of two albums. I have included each of the original album covers so that you can see the inspiration.

Rest in Miami - 2020

This piece was made in phtoshop and was based on a pencil sketch I did beforehand.

It is done in the vaporwave/hotline miami artstyle and itends to show that no matter how rich you are in life, we are all worth the same in death.





Vaporwave Collages - Various Years

These are all personal projects that I have done over the years. I'm really fascinated with the vaporwave art style, so these are digital art pieces that fall under that aesthetic. These were all done in photoshop usiing drawing tools, pictures from the internet, and blending options. Each of them took anywhere from an hour to nearly 6 hours to finish.

"Cityscape" - 2020

This is a digital painting that was constructed throughout the course of 2020. Starting with a pencil sketch, I scanned it in to Photoshop and began drawing over it using a Wacom drawing pad. Using over 50 different layers of painting and digital images, the end result is a desolate cityscape in a world far from our own.
