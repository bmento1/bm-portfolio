Random Works

A random collection of graphic design pieces that I was commissioned to make.

Babbis Beats Cover Art

A collection of cover art for a producer named Babbis. These were all made for a series of beats he made. He would provide me with the title and I would design these based off of that one word.
In order these are : Anger, Babbis Logo, Crypto, Harley Quinn, MDMA and Tense.

Pride Event Poster

This is a poster I was commissioned to make by Plymouth Pride. It was for their annual event hosted during pride month. It was shared on all of their social media platforms as well as handed out as a flyer in various locations.
You can check out their website here. They promote equality and inclusivity through their events and volunteering, while fostering education, awareness, respect, and visibility for everyone in the LGBTQ+ community.
