Photography

A collection of my photographs taken throughout the last 5 years.

Rubik's Cube Still Life - 2020

A still life series I took for my Into to Photography class in 2020. These are my Rubiks cubes that I arranged and photographed in my dorm.

Into to Photography Portraits - 2020

These are some portraits done for my Intro to Digital Photo class I took in Spring of 2020. These fine looking gentleman are my two closest friends from college, so I decided to shoot their portraits.

Into to Photography Final - 2020

These are my 8 favorite images submitted for my Intro to Digital Photography final in Spring of 2020. My theme for this project was 'the end of the world' and I wanted to focus on the decay of human structures. It was right at the beggining of the pandemic, so I wanted to capture images that encapsulated an apocolyptic atmosphere. These were taken in Weymouth, MA at an abandoned Air Force base.

Personal Camping Photos - 2020

These are images from a camping trip I took in May 2020 with my friends. We traveled to the abandoned Lyndonville Air Force base in Vermont. Spendiing 2 nights there, we got to bask in the amazing views. While these photos are great, they will never be able to encapsulate the true view we got to experience. You can view more here on my Flickr.

Mt. Lafayette, NH - 2019

These are some of the images from a hiking trip I took in August 2019 with my friends. We hiked 12.2 miles along the Falling Waters Trail, which took us to the peak of Mt.Lafayette. You can view more here on my Flickr.

Purchase Collage Landscapes 2019

Landscapes taken of SUNY Purchase in the winter of 2019.
