About

Hi I'm Brock!
I'm a 22 year old artist from Massachusetts with focuses on digital art and new media.

I graduated from SUNY Purchase with a Bachalors degree in New Media. My major encapsulated a wide variety of digital art mediums. This included graphic design, video production, photography, coding in html, css, and java as well as work done with 3D design and sound art. While I have a good grasp on the entire Adobe Suite, my strongest skills are in Photoshop and Premier Pro.

While I haven't worked on any major projects since graduating, my passion for creating visually apealing works of art remains the same. I hope to pursue a career where I am given the freedom to show off my creative and technical skills. In the future I would love to grow my video production and graphic design knowlege through hands on, industry experience.
